## What I do
- Analyze protein sequences and structures
- Work with mass spectrometry data
- Study protein-protein interactions
- Perform protein folding and structure prediction
- Analyze post-translational modifications
- Use tools like AlphaFold, MODELLER, PyMOL

## When to use me
When analyzing protein data, proteomics experiments, or structural biology.

- Mass spectrometry
- Protein sequencing
- Protein structure prediction
- Protein-protein interaction networks
